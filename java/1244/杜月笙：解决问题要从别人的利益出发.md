$$

$$

使用拉格朗日乘数法，引入拉格朗日乘子 $\lambda$，构造拉格朗日函数：

$$

$$

\frac{\partial L}{\partial f_B} = 1 - \lambda = 0 \\
\frac{\partial L}{\partial \lambda} = F - f_A - f_B = 0
