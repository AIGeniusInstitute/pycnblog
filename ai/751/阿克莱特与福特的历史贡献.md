**Industrial Revolution**:
An economic transformation that emerged in the late 18th to early 19th century in the UK, characterized by mechanized production that improved efficiency and promoted industrialization and urbanization.

**Automation**:
The use of machines and control systems to perform tasks that were previously done by hand, to increase production efficiency and quality.

**Digital factory**:
A factory that is fully digitized using technologies like the Internet of Things and cloud computing to improve production efficiency and decision accuracy.

**Smart manufacturing**:
Combining artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies to make the manufacturing process more intelligent, allowing for the autonomous optimization of production processes.
